Our history
ARS was created in December 2016 by founder Karlo Vidakovic in Croatia capital city, Zagreb. In 2010 he decided to move to Sillicon Valley where he worked at Google and Facebook before embarking on his ARS adventure. He was appointed as CEO and Board Member in ARS from 2016 till now.
